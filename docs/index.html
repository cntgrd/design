Design is critical to Centigrade.

We underwent a rigorous, iterative, and democratic process to design our brand identity.

These are all the prototypes we went through before our final logotype.

We started with Futura, a geometric font, and a blue-red gradient accent.

However, we wanted something simpler and more versatile.

We made some guiding decisions about the brand:

Calibre is the font of Centigrade.

Dodger Blue is the color of Centigrade.

#008AFF

Our final version is in Calibre Semi-Bold with a degree-symbol accent (Calibre Regular) in our Dodger Blue.

It drops its color in certain contexts,

and flips to white in dark or complex contexts.

Designing an icon that would work in compact contexts was similarly iterative.

Our final icon matches the logotype in palette and simplicity, but is vibrant enough to stand out on a mobile screen.
